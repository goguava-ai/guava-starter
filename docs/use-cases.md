# Guava Use Cases by Vertical

## Healthcare

1. **Appointment Reminders & Scheduling** — Outbound calls to remind patients of upcoming appointments, reschedule cancellations, or fill last-minute openings on the calendar.
2. **Post-Discharge Follow-Up** — Call recently discharged patients to check on recovery, confirm medication adherence, and flag concerns for clinical review.
3. **Prescription Refill Coordination** — Proactively reach out when a refill is due, confirm the prescription, and route to a pharmacist if questions arise.
4. **Insurance Pre-Authorization** — Outbound calls to insurance carriers to gather pre-authorization details and collect structured approval data before a procedure.
5. **Patient Intake & Medical History Collection** — Conduct pre-visit intake calls to collect symptoms, allergies, and insurance information before the patient arrives.

---

## Financial Services & Banking

1. **Loan Application Follow-Up** — Reach out to applicants to collect missing documentation, answer eligibility questions, and move applications through the pipeline.
2. **Fraud Alert Verification** — Call cardholders to verify suspicious transactions and confirm whether activity should be allowed or blocked.
3. **Account Delinquency Outreach** — Conduct early-stage collections calls, offer payment arrangements, and collect structured commitment-to-pay data.
4. **Customer Onboarding** — Walk new customers through account setup, required disclosures, and initial product configuration over the phone.
5. **Investment Satisfaction Surveys** — Poll clients on advisor performance, portfolio satisfaction, and service quality to feed into CRM and compliance reporting.

---

## Insurance

1. **First Notice of Loss (FNOL)** — Accept inbound claims calls, collect structured loss details, and create a ticket without requiring a live adjuster.
2. **Policy Renewal Outreach** — Call policyholders approaching renewal, confirm coverage needs, and flag upsell or retention opportunities.
3. **Underwriting Information Collection** — Outbound calls to gather the supplemental information needed to underwrite a new policy application.
4. **Claims Status Updates** — Proactively call claimants with status updates and field questions about next steps without adjuster involvement.
5. **Risk Survey & Inspection Scheduling** — Coordinate property inspection appointments and collect pre-inspection risk data from the insured.

---

## Real Estate

1. **Lead Qualification** — Call inbound inquiry leads to qualify intent, budget, and timeline before routing to a human agent.
2. **Showing Scheduling** — Outbound calls to interested buyers to schedule and confirm property showings, syncing with agent calendars.
3. **Tenant Maintenance Requests** — Accept inbound maintenance calls, collect issue details and urgency level, and create structured work orders.
4. **Lease Renewal Outreach** — Contact tenants ahead of lease expiration to gauge renewal intent and collect updated contact and income information.
5. **Post-Close Satisfaction Survey** — Survey buyers and sellers after closing to gather feedback for agent performance reviews and referral programs.

---

## Retail & E-Commerce

1. **Order Status & Delivery Updates** — Proactively call customers about delayed shipments, confirm delivery windows, and offer resolution options.
2. **Post-Purchase Feedback Surveys** — Collect structured product ratings and open-ended comments shortly after delivery to feed review systems.
3. **Abandoned Cart Recovery** — Outbound calls to high-value cart abandoners to offer assistance, answer product questions, or provide an incentive.
4. **Loyalty Program Enrollment** — Call recent first-time buyers to explain loyalty benefits and collect opt-in confirmation and preferences.
5. **Product Recall Notification** — Rapidly contact affected customers with recall details, safety instructions, and return or replacement options.

---

## Hospitality & Travel

1. **Reservation Confirmation & Upsell** — Confirm bookings via outbound call and offer room upgrades, early check-in, or add-on packages.
2. **Pre-Arrival Preference Collection** — Call guests before arrival to collect dietary restrictions, accessibility needs, and special requests.
3. **Post-Stay Satisfaction Survey** — Follow up after checkout to gather structured ratings on cleanliness, service, and amenities.
4. **Cancellation Winback** — Reach out to recently cancelled bookings to understand the reason, offer alternatives, and attempt to recover the reservation.
5. **Flight or Itinerary Disruption Handling** — Proactively call affected travelers when a disruption occurs, collect rebooking preferences, and confirm new arrangements.

---

## Automotive

1. **Service Reminder & Appointment Booking** — Outbound calls to customers due for routine maintenance to book a service bay appointment.
2. **Recall Notification & Scheduling** — Contact affected vehicle owners about safety recalls and collect scheduling preferences for the repair.
3. **Post-Service Follow-Up** — Call customers after a service visit to confirm satisfaction, address any unresolved issues, and request a review.
4. **Lease-End Outreach** — Contact lessees approaching end-of-term to discuss return, buyout, or re-lease options and collect a decision.
5. **Financing Pre-Qualification** — Conduct outbound calls to sales leads to collect income and credit background information before the dealership visit.

---

## Logistics & Supply Chain

1. **Delivery Confirmation & Signature Collection** — Call recipients ahead of delivery to confirm availability, collect access instructions, and record verbal confirmation.
2. **Carrier Check-In & ETA Updates** — Automated outbound calls to drivers or carriers to collect load status and update shipper ETAs in the TMS.
3. **Damaged Goods Claims Intake** — Accept inbound calls from recipients reporting damage, collect structured claim details, and create a case.
4. **Customs & Compliance Information Collection** — Outbound calls to shippers or brokers to gather missing documentation details required for clearance.
5. **Proof of Delivery Disputes** — Inbound call handling for delivery disputes, collecting structured incident details and routing to the appropriate resolution team.

---

## Legal & Professional Services

1. **Intake & Conflict Check** — Conduct initial client intake calls to collect matter details, check for conflicts of interest, and qualify the prospect before attorney time is spent.
2. **Deposition & Hearing Scheduling** — Coordinate availability across multiple parties and confirm scheduled dates and logistics.
3. **Document Collection Follow-Up** — Outbound calls to clients or opposing parties to follow up on outstanding document requests and log responses.
4. **Client Status Updates** — Proactively call clients with case status updates to reduce inbound inquiry volume to attorneys and paralegals.
5. **Billing & Invoice Follow-Up** — Reach out on outstanding invoices, confirm receipt, and collect payment commitments or dispute details.

---

## Government & Public Sector

1. **Benefits Enrollment Outreach** — Call eligible residents to inform them of available benefits programs and guide them through enrollment questions.
2. **Permit & License Application Follow-Up** — Contact applicants with missing information needed to process a permit or license application.
3. **Public Health Surveys** — Conduct structured health surveys across a population to gather epidemiological data.
4. **Utility Shutoff Warning & Payment Arrangement** — Notify residents of impending shutoffs, offer payment plans, and collect structured commitments.
5. **Emergency & Public Safety Notifications** — Outbound notification calls for weather events, evacuations, or public safety alerts with structured confirmation collection.

---

## Education

1. **Enrollment & Registration Follow-Up** — Reach out to applicants or returning students with incomplete enrollment paperwork to collect missing details.
2. **Tuition Payment Reminders** — Proactively call students or guardians before payment deadlines and offer structured payment plan arrangements.
3. **Attendance & Absence Check-In** — Automated outbound calls to parents or guardians when a student has an unexcused absence to collect a reason.
4. **Alumni Fundraising Outreach** — Conduct fundraising calls to alumni, collect giving amounts, and capture pledge commitments.
5. **Course Feedback Surveys** — Post-term calls to students to gather structured ratings and open-ended comments on courses and instructors.

---

## Utilities & Energy

1. **Outage Status & Restoration Updates** — Proactively call affected customers during an outage with status information and estimated restoration time.
2. **Smart Meter Enrollment** — Outbound calls to eligible customers to explain smart meter benefits and schedule an installation appointment.
3. **High-Usage Alerts & Conservation Tips** — Call customers whose usage is trending significantly above normal to alert them and offer efficiency guidance.
4. **Service Connection Scheduling** — Coordinate new service activation appointments with customers moving into a service area.
5. **Satisfaction & Net Promoter Surveys** — Post-interaction surveys to measure customer satisfaction and collect structured NPS data.

---

## Telecommunications

1. **Churn Prevention Outreach** — Identify at-risk subscribers and conduct outbound calls to surface pain points and offer retention options.
2. **Plan Upgrade & Upsell** — Call customers on legacy plans to explain new offerings and collect upgrade decisions.
3. **Technical Support Pre-Triage** — Inbound call handling that collects structured troubleshooting information before routing to a live technician.
4. **Number Porting Coordination** — Outbound calls to customers mid-port to confirm details, collect missing account information, and manage expectations.
5. **Device Trade-In & Upgrade Campaigns** — Reach out to customers eligible for a device upgrade to collect trade-in interest and schedule a store visit or ship-in.
