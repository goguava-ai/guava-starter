KEYSTONE PROPERTY & CASUALTY
PREMIUM DISCOUNTS AND PRICING GUIDE

PREMIUM CALCULATION OVERVIEW

Your annual premium is determined by a base rate applied to your Coverage A (dwelling)
limit, adjusted by a series of multiplicative rating factors. The formula is:

  Annual Premium = (Coverage A / $1,000) x Base Rate x Combined Rating Factor
                   + Liability Premium + Endorsement Premiums - Discounts

Base rates vary by risk tier:
  - Tier 1 (Preferred): $0.32 per $1,000 of Coverage A
    Newer construction, superior materials, low-risk territory, excellent claims history
  - Tier 2 (Standard): $0.48 per $1,000 of Coverage A
    Average construction, moderate-risk territory, typical claims history
  - Tier 3 (Non-Standard): $0.78 per $1,000 of Coverage A
    Older construction, higher-risk territory, or prior claims activity

RATING FACTORS

Territory Factor (TF): Reflects geographic risk based on weather patterns, crime
statistics, fire protection, and legal environment.
  - Low risk: 0.85
  - Moderate risk: 1.00
  - High risk: 1.25
  - Coastal/hurricane-prone: 1.40

Construction Factor (CF):
  - Superior fire-resistive: 0.80
  - Masonry: 0.90
  - Frame: 1.00
  - Log or mixed non-standard: 1.15

Protection Class Factor (PF): Based on distance to nearest fire station and hydrant,
and quality of local fire department.
  - Protection class 1-3: 0.80
  - Protection class 4-6: 1.00
  - Protection class 7-8: 1.15
  - Protection class 9-10: 1.35

Age of Home Factor (AHF):
  - Built within last 5 years: 0.90
  - Built 6-15 years ago: 1.00
  - Built 16-30 years ago: 1.10
  - Built over 30 years ago: 1.20
  (Factor may be reduced if major systems — roof, electrical, plumbing, HVAC — have
  been updated within the last 10 years.)

Deductible Factor (DF):
  - $500 deductible: 1.12
  - $1,000 deductible: 1.00
  - $2,500 deductible: 0.88
  - $5,000 deductible: 0.78

Claims History Factor (CHF):
  - No claims in 5+ years: 0.92
  - No claims in 3-5 years: 1.00
  - One claim in last 3 years: 1.10
  - Two claims in last 3 years: 1.30
  - Three or more claims in last 5 years: 1.50 (may trigger underwriting review)

AVAILABLE DISCOUNTS

Multi-Policy Discount
Bundle your homeowners policy with a Keystone auto, umbrella, or landlord policy.
  - Home + auto: 15% discount
  - Home + auto + umbrella: 22% discount
  - Each additional qualifying policy: 3% incremental discount
Maximum combined multi-policy discount: 25%.

Protective Devices Discount
Install qualifying safety and security systems:
  - Smoke detectors (required in all rooms): 2%
  - Monitored fire alarm (central station): 5%
  - Monitored burglar alarm (central station): 5%
  - Automatic fire sprinkler system: 8%
  - Deadbolt locks on all exterior doors: 2%
  - Water leak detection system: 3%
Discounts are cumulative. Maximum protective devices discount: 18%.

New Home Discount
Homes built within the last 10 years qualify for a new home credit:
  - Built 0-3 years ago: 12%
  - Built 4-6 years ago: 8%
  - Built 7-10 years ago: 5%

Claims-Free Discount
Policyholders with no claims filed in the last 5 or more years:
  - 5-7 years claims-free: 8%
  - 8-10 years claims-free: 10%
  - 10+ years claims-free: 12%

Loyalty Discount
Continuous Keystone policyholders receive tenure-based credits:
  - 3-5 years: 3%
  - 6-10 years: 6%
  - 11+ years: 9%

Autopay and Paperless Discount
  - Enroll in automatic premium payments: 2%
  - Opt in to paperless billing and documents: 1%
  - Combined autopay + paperless: 3%

Senior Homeowner Discount
Policyholders aged 55 or older who are retired and occupy the home full-time:
  - 5% discount on property coverages

PREMIUM CALCULATION EXAMPLE

Assumptions:
  - Coverage A: $350,000 (Tier 2 base rate: $0.48 per $1,000)
  - Territory: Moderate (TF = 1.00)
  - Construction: Masonry (CF = 0.90)
  - Protection class 4 (PF = 1.00)
  - Built 8 years ago (AHF = 1.00)
  - $1,000 deductible (DF = 1.00)
  - No claims in 6 years (CHF = 0.92)

Step 1: Base dwelling premium
  $350,000 / $1,000 x $0.48 = $168.00

Step 2: Apply rating factors
  Combined factor = 1.00 x 0.90 x 1.00 x 1.00 x 1.00 x 0.92 = 0.828
  Modified dwelling premium = $168.00 x 0.828 = $139.10

Step 3: Add other coverage premiums
  Coverage B (10% of dwelling premium): $13.91
  Coverage C (40% of dwelling premium): $55.64
  Coverage D (20% of dwelling premium): $27.82
  Property subtotal: $236.47

Step 4: Add liability and endorsements
  Coverage E ($300,000): $115.00
  Coverage F ($5,000): $32.00
  Water backup ($10,000): $85.00
  Equipment breakdown: $55.00
  Liability/endorsement subtotal: $287.00

Step 5: Total before discounts
  $236.47 + $287.00 = $523.47

Step 6: Apply discounts
  Multi-policy (home + auto): 15%
  Protective devices (monitored fire + burglar alarm): 10%
  Claims-free (6 years): 8%
  Combined discount = 1 - (0.85 x 0.90 x 0.92) = 1 - 0.7038 = 29.62%
  Discount amount: $523.47 x 0.2962 = $155.05

Final annual premium: $523.47 - $155.05 = $368.42
