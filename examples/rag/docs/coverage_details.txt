KEYSTONE PROPERTY & CASUALTY
HOMEOWNERS INSURANCE POLICY - COVERAGE DETAILS
POLICY FORM HO-3 (SPECIAL FORM)
================================================================================

COVERAGE A - DWELLING
---------------------
Coverage A protects the physical structure of your home, including attached
structures such as an attached garage, deck, or porch. This coverage is based
on the estimated replacement cost of your home at the time the policy is written.

COVERED: Walls, roof, built-in appliances, installed flooring, plumbing,
electrical systems, HVAC systems, attached garage, permanent fixtures.

NOT COVERED: Land value, separate structures (see Coverage B), routine
maintenance or wear and tear, damage caused by insects or vermin.

Coverage A limit is the dwelling replacement cost stated on your declarations
page. Most Keystone policies include a 125% extended replacement cost
provision, meaning we will pay up to 125% of your Coverage A limit if
rebuilding costs exceed the stated amount due to market fluctuations.


COVERAGE B - OTHER STRUCTURES
-----------------------------
Coverage B covers structures on your property that are not attached to the
main dwelling. The standard limit is 10% of your Coverage A amount.

EXAMPLES: Detached garage, storage shed, fence, in-ground pool, guest house,
detached workshop, gazebo, mailbox, retaining walls.

NOT COVERED: Structures used for commercial purposes, structures rented to
non-tenants, any structure used for agricultural purposes.

If your Coverage A limit is $350,000, your Coverage B limit is $35,000.
Additional Coverage B limits may be purchased in increments of $10,000.


COVERAGE C - PERSONAL PROPERTY
------------------------------
Coverage C protects your personal belongings, whether they are in your home,
in your car, or anywhere in the world. The standard limit is 50% of your
Coverage A amount.

COVERED: Furniture, clothing, electronics, appliances, sporting goods, books,
kitchen items, tools, and most other personal possessions.

NOT COVERED: Motor vehicles, aircraft, animals, business property exceeding
$2,500 in value, data stored on electronic media.

SPECIAL SUBLIMITS APPLY to certain categories of personal property. See your
Deductibles and Limits schedule for details on sublimits for jewelry,
electronics, firearms, cash, and other restricted categories.

If your Coverage A limit is $350,000, your Coverage C limit is $175,000.
Personal property is covered at actual cash value unless you have purchased
the Replacement Cost endorsement (Form KPC-RC100).


COVERAGE D - LOSS OF USE
-------------------------
If a covered peril makes your home uninhabitable, Coverage D pays for
additional living expenses while your home is being repaired or rebuilt.
The standard limit is 20% of your Coverage A amount.

COVERED EXPENSES: Temporary housing (hotel, rental home), restaurant meals
above your normal food costs, laundry services, storage fees, pet boarding
if your temporary housing does not allow pets.

DURATION: Coverage D benefits are payable for up to 24 months or until your
home is repaired, whichever comes first. A 12-month extension may be granted
if delays are caused by permitting or contractor availability.

If your Coverage A limit is $350,000, your Coverage D limit is $70,000.


COVERAGE E - PERSONAL LIABILITY
-------------------------------
Coverage E provides liability protection if you are legally responsible for
bodily injury or property damage to others. The standard limit is $100,000
per occurrence.

COVERED: Injuries to guests on your property, damage caused by you or family
members to others' property, dog bite liability (breed restrictions apply),
legal defense costs (paid in addition to the liability limit).

NOT COVERED: Intentional acts, business activities, injuries to household
members, motor vehicle liability, watercraft over 26 feet in length.

Available limits: $100,000, $300,000, or $500,000 per occurrence.
Keystone recommends a personal umbrella policy for limits exceeding $500,000.


COVERAGE F - MEDICAL PAYMENTS TO OTHERS
----------------------------------------
Coverage F pays for medical expenses of persons accidentally injured on your
property or by your activities, regardless of fault. The standard limit is
$5,000 per person.

COVERED: Emergency room visits, ambulance costs, surgery, X-rays, dental
treatment resulting from an accident on your property.

NOT COVERED: Injuries to you or household members, injuries arising from
business activities, injuries to persons regularly residing on the property.

Available limits: $1,000, $2,500, or $5,000 per person.
Claims under Coverage F do not require a liability determination.
