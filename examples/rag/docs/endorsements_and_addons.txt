KEYSTONE PROPERTY & CASUALTY
ENDORSEMENTS AND OPTIONAL ADD-ONS GUIDE

SCHEDULED PERSONAL PROPERTY (KPC-SPP)
Open-peril, worldwide coverage for high-value items at individually agreed values.
No deductible applies. Items must be individually appraised and listed on the schedule.
Eligible categories: jewelry, fine art, musical instruments, cameras, antiques, coin
and stamp collections, and sports memorabilia. Annual premium is calculated at a rate
per $100 of scheduled value. Typical rates:
  - Jewelry: $1.10 per $100
  - Fine art: $0.45 per $100
  - Musical instruments: $0.65 per $100
  - Cameras and photography equipment: $0.80 per $100
  - Sports memorabilia and collectibles: $0.55 per $100
Reappraisal is required every 3 years or upon request. New items may be added mid-term
with a pro-rated premium adjustment.

WATER BACKUP AND SUMP OVERFLOW (KPC-WB)
Covers property damage caused by water that backs up through sewers, drains, or sump
pumps. This peril is excluded from the base HO-3 policy. Available sublimits:
  - $5,000 per occurrence: $45 annual premium
  - $10,000 per occurrence: $85 annual premium
  - $25,000 per occurrence: $160 annual premium
Policyholders with basements or finished lower levels are strongly encouraged to add
this endorsement. A $500 deductible applies separately from the base policy deductible.

EQUIPMENT BREAKDOWN (KPC-EB)
Covers sudden and accidental mechanical or electrical breakdown of home systems and
major appliances, including HVAC, water heaters, electrical panels, built-in kitchen
appliances, and well pumps. Covers the cost to repair or replace the failed equipment
plus any resulting damage to the dwelling or personal property. Annual flat premium
ranges from $40 to $85 depending on home age and Coverage A amount. Excludes equipment
under manufacturer warranty or separate service contract.

IDENTITY THEFT EXPENSE COVERAGE (KPC-ID)
Reimburses the insured for out-of-pocket expenses incurred in restoring their identity
after a confirmed identity theft event. Covered expenses include:
  - Notary and certified mailing costs
  - Lost wages (up to $250 per day, 5 days maximum)
  - Attorney fees for identity restoration (up to $2,500)
  - Credit monitoring services (up to 24 months)
  - Filing fees and document replacement costs
Coverage limit: $15,000 per occurrence. No deductible. Annual premium: $25-$40.
Does not cover direct financial losses from fraud — only restoration expenses.

SERVICE LINE COVERAGE (KPC-SL)
Covers repair or replacement of underground service lines running from the public
utility connection or property boundary to the dwelling. Covered lines include water,
sewer, electrical, natural gas, and communications cables. Coverage applies to damage
from wear and tear, rust, corrosion, tree root intrusion, and mechanical breakdown —
causes that are otherwise excluded from the base policy. Limit: $10,000 per occurrence.
Deductible: $500. Annual premium: $30-$55.

REPLACEMENT COST ON PERSONAL PROPERTY (KPC-RC)
Upgrades the loss settlement basis for Coverage C (Personal Property) from actual cash
value to full replacement cost. Without this endorsement, depreciation is deducted from
personal property claims, which can significantly reduce the payout on older items.
With this endorsement, Keystone pays the full cost to replace damaged items with new
items of like kind and quality. Annual premium increase: approximately 15-20% of the
base Coverage C premium. Strongly recommended for policyholders with furnishings or
electronics more than 3 years old.

EARTHQUAKE COVERAGE (KPC-EQ)
Separate endorsement providing coverage for damage caused by earthquake, volcanic
eruption, and related earth movement. Features a percentage-based deductible applied
to Coverage A:
  - Zone 1 (low seismic risk): 5% of Coverage A, annual premium $75-$150
  - Zone 2 (moderate seismic risk): 10% of Coverage A, annual premium $250-$500
  - Zone 3 (high seismic risk): 15% of Coverage A, annual premium $800-$2,000
Masonry veneer surcharge of 20% applies. Homes built before 1970 without seismic
retrofitting may be subject to additional underwriting review. Covers dwelling, other
structures, and personal property. Does not cover land, landscaping, or external
paving.

FLOOD COVERAGE (KPC-FL)
Available as a companion policy or endorsement, depending on the flood zone
designation. Keystone partners with the National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP) for
properties in designated high-risk zones. Private flood options are available for
properties in moderate-to-low risk zones. Typical limits:
  - Building coverage: up to $250,000
  - Contents coverage: up to $100,000
Waiting period: 30 days from date of purchase (NFIP). Private flood policies may
have shorter waiting periods. Contact your Keystone agent for a flood risk assessment
and premium quote.

HOME BUSINESS ENDORSEMENT (KPC-HB)
Extends coverage for business equipment and inventory kept at the insured premises
beyond the standard $2,500 sublimit. Available limits:
  - $5,000: $60 annual premium
  - $10,000: $110 annual premium
  - $25,000: $225 annual premium
Also provides $300,000 in business liability coverage for third-party bodily injury
or property damage arising from business activities conducted at the home. Does not
cover professional errors and omissions, cyberliability, or commercial auto exposures.
Eligible for home offices, craft businesses, and small-scale e-commerce operations.
