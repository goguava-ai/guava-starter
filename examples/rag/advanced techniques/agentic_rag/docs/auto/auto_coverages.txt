KEYSTONE INSURANCE GROUP
AUTO INSURANCE COVERAGES
========================================

OVERVIEW
Keystone Insurance Group offers comprehensive auto insurance protection
tailored to your needs. All policies include 24/7 claims support and
flexible payment options. Coverage is available in all 50 states.

----------------------------------------
LIABILITY COVERAGE
----------------------------------------
Bodily Injury Liability:
  - Standard limits: $100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident
  - Optional higher limits: $250,000/$500,000 or $500,000/$1,000,000
  - Covers medical expenses, lost wages, and legal fees for others
    when you are at fault in an accident
  - Pricing: $100K/$300K starts at $280/six months

Property Damage Liability:
  - Standard limit: $100,000 per accident
  - Optional limits: $50,000 or $250,000
  - Covers damage to other vehicles, structures, fences, and property
  - Pricing: $100K limit starts at $145/six months

----------------------------------------
COLLISION COVERAGE
----------------------------------------
  - Pays to repair or replace your vehicle after a collision,
    regardless of fault
  - Deductible options: $250, $500, $750, $1,000
  - Pricing varies by deductible:
      $250 deductible: approx. $385/six months
      $500 deductible: approx. $310/six months
      $750 deductible: approx. $260/six months
      $1,000 deductible: approx. $215/six months
  - Includes coverage for single-vehicle accidents (e.g., hitting
    a guardrail or pothole damage)

----------------------------------------
COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE
----------------------------------------
  - Covers non-collision events: theft, vandalism, hail, flood,
    fire, falling objects, animal strikes, and glass breakage
  - Deductible options: $100, $250, $500, $1,000
  - Pricing varies by deductible:
      $100 deductible: approx. $195/six months
      $250 deductible: approx. $160/six months
      $500 deductible: approx. $120/six months
      $1,000 deductible: approx. $85/six months
  - Full glass coverage available with $0 deductible for
    windshield repairs (add $25/six months)

----------------------------------------
UNINSURED/UNDERINSURED MOTORIST COVERAGE
----------------------------------------
  - Bodily injury: $100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident
  - Property damage: $50,000 per accident
  - Stacking available in eligible states for multi-vehicle policies
  - Pricing: starts at $95/six months
  - Protects you when the at-fault driver has no insurance or
    insufficient coverage to pay for your damages

----------------------------------------
MEDICAL PAYMENTS COVERAGE
----------------------------------------
  - Covers medical and funeral expenses for you and your passengers,
    regardless of fault
  - Limits available: $1,000, $5,000, $10,000, $25,000
  - Pricing: $5,000 limit starts at $30/six months
  - No deductible applies

----------------------------------------
RENTAL REIMBURSEMENT
----------------------------------------
  - Covers the cost of a rental car while your vehicle is being
    repaired due to a covered claim
  - Daily limits: $30/day, $40/day, or $50/day
  - Maximum rental period: 30 days per claim
  - Pricing: $30/day option starts at $18/six months

----------------------------------------
ROADSIDE ASSISTANCE
----------------------------------------
  - Towing up to 25 miles to nearest qualified repair facility
  - Battery jump-start and tire change service
  - Lockout assistance (up to $75 per occurrence)
  - Emergency fuel delivery (up to 2 gallons)
  - Pricing: $12/six months per vehicle
  - Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
  - No limit on the number of service calls per policy period
