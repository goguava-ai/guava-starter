KEYSTONE INSURANCE GROUP
HOMEOWNERS INSURANCE ENDORSEMENTS
========================================

OVERVIEW
Endorsements allow you to customize your Keystone HO-3 homeowners
policy with additional coverage beyond the standard policy. The
following endorsements are available to all Keystone policyholders.

----------------------------------------
SCHEDULED PERSONAL PROPERTY
----------------------------------------
  - Provides coverage for high-value items that exceed the standard
    sub-limits on your HO-3 policy
  - Items must be individually appraised and listed on the schedule
  - Coverage is on an agreed-value basis with no deductible
  - Covers accidental loss, theft, and mysterious disappearance
  - Eligible categories:
      Jewelry and watches
      Fine art and antiques
      Musical instruments
      Camera equipment
      Collectibles (coins, stamps, sports memorabilia)
      Furs
  - Pricing: approximately $1.50 per $100 of insured value per year
  - Example: A $10,000 engagement ring costs roughly $150/year
  - Appraisal must be dated within the last 3 years

----------------------------------------
WATER BACKUP AND SUMP OVERFLOW
----------------------------------------
  - Covers damage caused by water that backs up through sewers,
    drains, or sump pump failure
  - This peril is excluded from the standard HO-3 policy
  - Coverage limits available: $5,000, $10,000, $25,000, $50,000
  - Pricing:
      $5,000 limit: $45/year
      $10,000 limit: $65/year
      $25,000 limit: $110/year
      $50,000 limit: $175/year
  - Subject to a $500 deductible
  - Strongly recommended for homes with basements or in areas
    with aging municipal sewer systems

----------------------------------------
EQUIPMENT BREAKDOWN
----------------------------------------
  - Covers the repair or replacement of home systems and appliances
    that fail due to mechanical or electrical breakdown
  - Covered equipment includes:
      HVAC systems
      Water heaters
      Electrical panels
      Built-in appliances (dishwashers, ovens, etc.)
      Well pumps and septic systems
  - Coverage limit: $50,000 per occurrence
  - Includes spoilage coverage up to $500 for food loss due to
    refrigerator or freezer breakdown
  - Pricing: $40/year
  - Deductible: $250 per occurrence

----------------------------------------
IDENTITY THEFT RESTORATION
----------------------------------------
  - Provides up to $25,000 in expense reimbursement for costs
    related to restoring your identity after theft or fraud
  - Covered expenses include:
      Lost wages (up to $500/week for 4 weeks)
      Legal fees
      Notary and certified mailing costs
      Credit monitoring for 12 months
      Loan re-application fees
  - Includes access to a dedicated identity restoration specialist
  - Pricing: $30/year
  - No deductible applies

----------------------------------------
HOME BUSINESS COVERAGE
----------------------------------------
  - Extends coverage for business property and liability beyond
    the limited protection in the standard HO-3 policy
  - Business property coverage: up to $10,000 on premises and
    $5,000 away from premises
  - Business liability coverage: $300,000 per occurrence
  - Covers business equipment, inventory, supplies, and documents
  - Excludes professional liability (errors and omissions)
  - Pricing: $150/year
  - Deductible: $500 for property claims
  - Ideal for home-based consultants, freelancers, and small
    online business operators

----------------------------------------
BUNDLING ENDORSEMENTS
----------------------------------------
  - Add 2 or more endorsements and receive a 10% discount on the
    combined endorsement premiums
  - Add 4 or more endorsements and receive a 15% discount
  - Endorsements can be added at any time during your policy period;
    premiums will be prorated for the remaining term
