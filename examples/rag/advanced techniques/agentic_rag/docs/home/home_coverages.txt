KEYSTONE INSURANCE GROUP
HOMEOWNERS INSURANCE COVERAGES (HO-3 POLICY)
========================================

OVERVIEW
The Keystone HO-3 Special Form homeowners policy provides broad
coverage for your home and belongings. This is our most popular
homeowners policy, covering your dwelling against all perils
except those specifically excluded.

----------------------------------------
COVERAGE A - DWELLING
----------------------------------------
  - Covers the physical structure of your home, including attached
    structures such as a garage or deck
  - Insured for the full replacement cost of the home
  - Minimum coverage: $150,000
  - No upper limit on dwelling coverage
  - Covers perils including fire, wind, hail, lightning, explosion,
    aircraft, vehicles, smoke, vandalism, and theft
  - Excluded perils: flood, earthquake, neglect, war, nuclear hazard
  - Deductible options: $1,000, $2,500, $5,000, $10,000
  - Higher deductibles reduce premiums by 10% to 30%

----------------------------------------
COVERAGE B - OTHER STRUCTURES
----------------------------------------
  - Covers detached structures: sheds, fences, detached garages,
    guest houses, and swimming pools
  - Default limit: 10% of Coverage A amount
  - Can be increased up to 20% for an additional $45/year
  - Example: If dwelling coverage is $350,000, other structures
    default limit is $35,000

----------------------------------------
COVERAGE C - PERSONAL PROPERTY
----------------------------------------
  - Covers furniture, electronics, clothing, appliances, and other
    personal belongings
  - Default limit: 50% of Coverage A amount
  - Can be increased up to 75% for an additional $85/year
  - Sub-limits apply to certain categories:
      Jewelry and watches: $1,500
      Silverware and goldware: $2,500
      Firearms: $2,500
      Business property on premises: $2,500
      Cash and securities: $200
  - Higher limits available through scheduled personal property
    endorsement (see endorsements document)

----------------------------------------
COVERAGE D - LOSS OF USE
----------------------------------------
  - Pays for additional living expenses if your home is uninhabitable
    due to a covered loss
  - Default limit: 20% of Coverage A amount
  - Covers hotel stays, temporary rentals, meals, and other
    reasonable expenses above your normal costs
  - Coverage continues until the home is repaired or up to
    12 months, whichever comes first

----------------------------------------
COVERAGE E - PERSONAL LIABILITY
----------------------------------------
  - Protects against lawsuits for bodily injury or property damage
    to others caused by you or household members
  - Standard limit: $100,000 per occurrence
  - Optional higher limits: $300,000 or $500,000
  - Pricing: $300,000 limit adds $35/year; $500,000 adds $60/year
  - Covers legal defense costs in addition to the liability limit
  - Umbrella policy available for additional protection up to
    $1,000,000 or more

----------------------------------------
COVERAGE F - MEDICAL PAYMENTS TO OTHERS
----------------------------------------
  - Covers medical expenses for guests injured on your property,
    regardless of fault
  - Standard limit: $5,000 per person
  - Optional limit: $10,000 per person (adds $15/year)
  - Does not apply to injuries to household members

----------------------------------------
REPLACEMENT COST VS. ACTUAL CASH VALUE
----------------------------------------
Replacement Cost (included in standard HO-3):
  - Pays the full cost to repair or replace damaged property with
    materials of similar kind and quality
  - No deduction for depreciation
  - Applies to the dwelling (Coverage A) by default

Actual Cash Value (ACV):
  - Pays replacement cost minus depreciation
  - Applies to personal property (Coverage C) by default
  - Replacement cost on personal property can be added for
    approximately $40/year

----------------------------------------
INFLATION GUARD ENDORSEMENT
----------------------------------------
  - Automatically increases your dwelling coverage limit each year
    to keep pace with rising construction costs
  - Standard adjustment: 4% annually
  - Optional 6% adjustment available for an additional $20/year
  - Included at no extra charge for the 4% tier with all new
    Keystone HO-3 policies
  - Ensures you are not underinsured as rebuilding costs increase
