KEYSTONE INSURANCE GROUP
WHOLE LIFE INSURANCE PRODUCT GUIDE

OVERVIEW

Whole life insurance provides permanent coverage that lasts your entire lifetime, as long
as premiums are paid. Unlike term life, whole life builds cash value over time that you
can borrow against or withdraw. Premiums are fixed and guaranteed never to increase.

COVERAGE AMOUNTS

Keystone offers whole life policies from $25,000 to $1,000,000. The death benefit is
guaranteed and will never decrease. Coverage amounts above $500,000 may require a
medical exam and financial underwriting.

MONTHLY PREMIUM EXAMPLES (NON-SMOKER, STANDARD HEALTH)

  Age 25: $100,000 coverage = $85/month | $250,000 = $195/month
  Age 35: $100,000 coverage = $115/month | $250,000 = $270/month
  Age 45: $100,000 coverage = $170/month | $250,000 = $400/month
  Age 55: $100,000 coverage = $265/month | $250,000 = $625/month

Premiums are guaranteed level for life and will never increase.

CASH VALUE ACCUMULATION

Your whole life policy builds guaranteed cash value on a fixed schedule. A portion of
each premium payment goes into the cash value account, which grows tax-deferred.

Approximate cash value as a percentage of total premiums paid:
  Year 5:   15-20% of total premiums paid
  Year 10:  35-45% of total premiums paid
  Year 15:  55-65% of total premiums paid
  Year 20:  70-80% of total premiums paid
  Year 30:  90-100% of total premiums paid

Cash value is guaranteed by Keystone and is not subject to market fluctuations.

POLICY LOANS

You may borrow against your cash value at any time without credit checks or approval
processes. Key details:
  - Borrow up to 90% of your current cash value
  - Current loan interest rate: 5.5% annually
  - No required repayment schedule - pay back on your terms
  - Unpaid loans and interest reduce the death benefit
  - Loan interest compounds annually

Example: A policy with $50,000 cash value allows a loan of up to $45,000.

DIVIDENDS

Keystone whole life policies are participating, meaning they may earn annual dividends.
Dividends are not guaranteed but Keystone has paid dividends every year for the past
42 consecutive years. Current dividend rate: 4.2%.

Dividend options:
  - Cash payment: receive a check or direct deposit
  - Premium reduction: apply dividends to reduce your premium payment
  - Paid-up additions: purchase additional paid-up insurance, increasing both the
    death benefit and cash value
  - Accumulate at interest: leave dividends with Keystone to earn interest (currently 3.1%)

PAID-UP ADDITIONS

Paid-up additions (PUAs) are small blocks of additional whole life insurance purchased
with dividends or optional additional premium payments. PUAs increase both your death
benefit and cash value immediately, and they generate their own dividends, creating a
compounding growth effect.

SURRENDER VALUE

If you cancel your whole life policy, you receive the net cash surrender value. During
the first 10 years, a surrender charge may apply:
  Year 1-3:   surrender charge = 25% of cash value
  Year 4-6:   surrender charge = 15% of cash value
  Year 7-10:  surrender charge = 5% of cash value
  Year 11+:   no surrender charge

RIDERS AND OPTIONS

  - Waiver of Premium: If you become totally disabled before age 60, Keystone waives
    all premium payments while disability continues. Cost: $3-8/month.
  - Accelerated Death Benefit: Access up to 50% of the death benefit if diagnosed with
    a terminal illness (life expectancy under 12 months). No additional cost.
  - Child Term Rider: Covers all eligible children for $10,000-$25,000 each until age 25.
    Cost: $5-10/month regardless of number of children.
  - Guaranteed Insurability: Purchase additional coverage at specified future dates
    without evidence of insurability. Cost: $2-5/month.
