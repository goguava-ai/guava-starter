KEYSTONE INSURANCE GROUP
TERM LIFE INSURANCE
========================================

OVERVIEW
Keystone Term Life Insurance provides affordable, straightforward
life insurance protection for a specified period. It is designed
to cover your most critical financial obligations, such as a
mortgage, children's education, or income replacement.

----------------------------------------
TERM LENGTH OPTIONS
----------------------------------------
  - 10-Year Term: Ideal for short-term needs such as covering a
    business loan or bridging to retirement
  - 20-Year Term: Our most popular option, suitable for families
    with young children or new mortgages
  - 30-Year Term: Maximum protection period, recommended for new
    parents or those with long-term financial obligations

----------------------------------------
COVERAGE AMOUNTS
----------------------------------------
  - Minimum: $100,000
  - Maximum: $2,000,000
  - Coverage available in $50,000 increments
  - Coverage amount is level for the entire term
  - No medical exam required for amounts up to $500,000 (subject
    to health questionnaire approval)

----------------------------------------
CONVERTIBILITY
----------------------------------------
  - All Keystone term policies can be converted to a permanent
    whole life policy without a new medical exam
  - Conversion is available during the first 75% of the original
    term (e.g., within the first 15 years of a 20-year term)
  - Converted policy premiums are based on your attained age at
    the time of conversion
  - No conversion fees apply
  - Full cash value accumulation begins on the conversion date

----------------------------------------
ACCELERATED DEATH BENEFIT RIDER
----------------------------------------
  - Included at no additional cost with all Keystone term policies
  - Allows you to access up to 75% of the death benefit (maximum
    $1,000,000) if diagnosed with a qualifying terminal illness
    with a life expectancy of 12 months or less
  - Benefit is paid as a lump sum
  - Remaining death benefit is reduced by the amount accessed
  - No restrictions on how the funds are used
  - A one-time administrative fee of $150 applies at the time
    of the accelerated benefit claim

----------------------------------------
ADDITIONAL FEATURES
----------------------------------------
  - Premiums are guaranteed level for the entire term
  - 30-day free look period after policy delivery
  - Policies are contestable for the first 2 years
  - Beneficiary changes can be made at any time at no charge
  - Spousal discount: 10% off when both spouses apply together
