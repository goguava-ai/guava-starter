MERIDIAN TECHNOLOGIES
EMPLOYEE BENEFITS GUIDE
Plan Year: January 1 – December 31, 2025

================================================================================

HEALTH INSURANCE

Meridian offers three medical plan options through BlueCross BlueShield.
Coverage is available for employees, spouses/domestic partners, and dependents
up to age 26.

  Plan A — High Deductible Health Plan (HDHP) with HSA
    Individual deductible: $1,500 | Family deductible: $3,000
    Out-of-pocket max (individual): $4,500 | (family): $9,000
    Preventive care: Covered 100% in-network
    Meridian HSA contribution: $750/year individual, $1,500/year family
    Employee bi-weekly premium: $28 individual / $78 family

  Plan B — PPO Select
    Individual deductible: $500 | Family deductible: $1,000
    Out-of-pocket max (individual): $3,500 | (family): $7,000
    Primary care visit copay: $25 | Specialist copay: $50
    Employee bi-weekly premium: $62 individual / $156 family

  Plan C — PPO Plus (richest coverage)
    Individual deductible: $250 | Family deductible: $500
    Out-of-pocket max (individual): $2,500 | (family): $5,000
    Primary care visit copay: $15 | Specialist copay: $30
    Employee bi-weekly premium: $98 individual / $240 family

All plans include prescription drug coverage. Generic drugs: $10 copay.
Preferred brand: $35 copay. Non-preferred brand: $65 copay.

Open Enrollment runs November 1–15 each year for the following plan year.
Qualifying life events (marriage, birth, job loss of spouse coverage) allow
a 30-day enrollment window outside of open enrollment.


DENTAL INSURANCE

Two dental plans are available through Delta Dental.

  Basic Plan: Preventive (100%), Basic restorative (80%), Major (50%)
    Annual maximum benefit: $1,500 per person
    Orthodontia: Not covered
    Employee bi-weekly premium: $6 individual / $18 family

  Enhanced Plan: Preventive (100%), Basic restorative (90%), Major (70%)
    Annual maximum benefit: $2,500 per person
    Orthodontia: 50%, $2,000 lifetime maximum (dependents under 19)
    Employee bi-weekly premium: $14 individual / $38 family


VISION INSURANCE

Vision coverage is provided through VSP.
  - Annual eye exam: Covered in full (in-network)
  - Frames: $200 allowance every 12 months
  - Lenses: Covered in full every 12 months
  - Contact lenses: $200 allowance in lieu of frames/lenses
  Employee bi-weekly premium: $4 individual / $10 family


401(k) RETIREMENT PLAN

Meridian offers a 401(k) plan through Fidelity Investments. Employees are
eligible to participate on their first day of employment.

Company Match: Meridian matches 100% of employee contributions up to 4% of
eligible compensation. The match is deposited each pay period and is
immediately vested — there is no vesting cliff.

2025 IRS Contribution Limits:
  - Under age 50: $23,500
  - Age 50 or older (catch-up): $31,000

Employees may choose from a broad range of mutual funds and index funds, plus
Fidelity's target-date retirement funds. To enroll or adjust contributions,
log in to NetBenefits at netbenefits.fidelity.com or call Fidelity at
1-800-343-3548.

Roth 401(k): Meridian's plan also allows Roth (after-tax) contributions. The
same annual limits apply across traditional and Roth contributions combined.


FLEXIBLE SPENDING ACCOUNTS (FSA)

  Healthcare FSA: Set aside pre-tax dollars for eligible medical, dental, and
    vision expenses not covered by insurance. 2025 limit: $3,200.
    $640 rollover is permitted; unused amounts above $640 are forfeited.

  Dependent Care FSA: Set aside pre-tax dollars for eligible dependent care
    expenses (daycare, preschool, after-school programs, elder care) for
    dependents that allow you to work. 2025 limit: $5,000 per household.
    Dependent Care FSA funds do not roll over; use it or lose it by March 15.

FSAs are available to employees enrolled in Plan B or Plan C. Employees
enrolled in the HDHP (Plan A) are not eligible for the Healthcare FSA but
may contribute to an HSA.


LIFE & DISABILITY INSURANCE

  Basic Life Insurance: Meridian provides life insurance equal to 2x annual
    base salary at no cost to employees, up to a maximum of $500,000.

  Supplemental Life Insurance: Employees may purchase additional life
    insurance in increments of 1x salary, up to 8x salary total. Guaranteed
    issue (no medical underwriting) for amounts up to 4x salary during
    initial enrollment. Spousal and dependent life coverage also available.

  Short-Term Disability (STD): 60% of weekly base salary for up to 13 weeks
    after a 7-day elimination period. Meridian-paid; no employee premium.

  Long-Term Disability (LTD): 60% of monthly base salary after STD ends,
    up to a maximum of $10,000/month. Continues until age 65 or return to
    work, whichever comes first. Meridian-paid; no employee premium.


WELLNESS & ADDITIONAL BENEFITS

  Wellness Reimbursement: Up to $600 per year reimbursed for eligible
    wellness expenses: gym memberships, fitness equipment (up to $300),
    personal training, meditation apps, or ergonomic home office equipment.
    Submit receipts through the Workday expense module.

  Employee Assistance Program (EAP): Free, confidential counseling and
    referral services for employees and household members. Up to 8 sessions
    per issue per year at no cost. Available 24/7 at 1-800-555-0199.

  Commuter Benefits: Pre-tax payroll deduction for transit passes and
    vanpool expenses up to $315/month. Parking benefit up to $315/month.

  Tuition Reimbursement: Up to $5,250 per calendar year for job-related
    courses or degree programs at accredited institutions. Requires manager
    approval before enrollment. Grade of B or better required for
    reimbursement. Two-year service commitment required after reimbursement.

  Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP): Purchase Meridian stock at a 15%
    discount through payroll deductions each offering period (6 months).
    Maximum contribution: $25,000 per year (IRS limit).
