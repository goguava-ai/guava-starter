GETTING STARTED

1. PLACEMENT
   Place the BrewMaster Pro on a flat, stable, heat-resistant surface. Leave at
   least 6 inches of clearance above and 2 inches on each side for ventilation.
   Do not place near the edge of a counter or under low cabinets that restrict
   the lid opening.

2. FIRST USE — RINSE CYCLE
   Before brewing coffee for the first time, run a full rinse cycle using plain
   water. Fill the reservoir to the MAX line, insert the empty filter basket
   (no coffee or paper filter), place the carafe, and press BREW. Discard the
   water when complete. This removes manufacturing residue and primes the system.

3. FILLING THE RESERVOIR
   Lift the reservoir straight up to remove it. Fill under the tap to anywhere
   between the MIN (4-cup) and MAX (12-cup) lines. Reattach by aligning the
   rear tabs and pressing firmly until it clicks into place.

4. ADDING COFFEE
   Open the top lid to access the filter basket. For best results use a medium
   grind. Recommended ratio: 1 level tablespoon per 6 oz of water (roughly
   1 scoop per 2 cups). Place the permanent gold-tone filter in the basket
   before adding grounds. Do not overfill — grounds should sit no higher than
   1/2 inch below the rim of the basket.

   Paper filters: #4 basket-style filters are compatible if preferred. Using
   paper filters will slightly reduce extraction speed but produces a cleaner cup.


CONTROL PANEL

  BREW         — Start a brew cycle; press again to pause mid-brew.
  STRENGTH     — Cycles through Regular, Bold, and Extra Bold brew strengths.
                 Bold and Extra Bold extend the bloom and steep time.
  TEMP         — Sets brew temperature: 185°F, 195°F (default), or 205°F.
  SCHEDULE     — Program a delayed brew start (see Scheduling section).
  CLEAN        — Initiates the descale cleaning cycle (see Maintenance).
  DISPLAY      — Backlit LCD shows clock, scheduled brew time, selected
                 strength, and temperature.

Adjustments to STRENGTH and TEMP must be made before pressing BREW. Settings
are retained between uses until changed.


BREW SETTINGS

Regular: Standard extraction time. Recommended for light and medium roasts.
Bold: 20% longer bloom period. Recommended for medium-dark and dark roasts.
Extra Bold: 35% longer bloom and a 2-minute pre-infusion step. Best for
  espresso-roast beans or coarsely ground coffee.

Temperature:
  185°F — Lower extraction, reduced acidity; suited for sensitive stomachs.
  195°F — Balanced default; ideal for most coffee types.
  205°F — Higher extraction for light roasts; maximizes flavor complexity.


SCHEDULING A BREW

1. Set the current time: Hold SCHEDULE for 3 seconds. Use + and - to set
   hours, then press SCHEDULE again to advance to minutes. Press SCHEDULE
   again to confirm.
2. Set the brew start time: Press SCHEDULE once. Use + and - to set the
   desired time. Press BREW to confirm. The display will show the scheduled
   time with a clock icon.
3. To cancel a scheduled brew, press SCHEDULE and then press BREW without
   changing the time, or press any other button within 5 seconds.

Ensure the reservoir is filled and coffee is loaded the night before. The unit
will not start a scheduled brew if the reservoir reads as empty.


THERMAL CARAFE

The BrewMaster Pro brews directly into the stainless-steel thermal carafe.
The carafe keeps coffee hot for up to 4 hours without a heating element,
preserving flavor better than a glass carafe on a warming plate.

  - Lock the carafe lid to pour; twist the handle clockwise to lock open.
  - Do not place the carafe in a microwave or on a stovetop.
  - The carafe is top-rack dishwasher safe. The outer stainless shell should
    be hand-washed.
  - Do not use abrasive cleaners on the carafe interior.


BREW PAUSE FEATURE

Pressing BREW during an active cycle pauses extraction for up to 30 seconds,
allowing you to pour a cup before brewing is complete. Brewing resumes
automatically. Do not leave the carafe out for more than 30 seconds — the unit
will stop brewing and display a CARAFE alert if the carafe is not replaced.


MAINTENANCE

Rinse the filter basket and permanent filter after every use. Once per week,
wash the filter basket, permanent filter, and reservoir with warm soapy water
or place them in the top rack of a dishwasher.

DESCALING: Mineral buildup reduces heating efficiency and can affect taste.
Descale every 60 brew cycles (the display shows a CLEAN reminder). Use the
Apex Descaling Solution (sold separately, model AX-DS-01) or white vinegar.

  Descale Procedure:
  1. Fill the reservoir with 32 oz of descaling solution mixed with 32 oz of
     water (or use 48 oz of undiluted white vinegar).
  2. Insert the empty filter basket with no filter or grounds.
  3. Place the carafe and press CLEAN.
  4. The cycle runs automatically in two phases (approx. 40 minutes total).
     Do not interrupt the cycle.
  5. Discard the contents of the carafe.
  6. Run two full brew cycles with plain water before making coffee.

The CLEAN reminder resets automatically after a completed descale cycle.
